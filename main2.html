About the System

A face recognition-based attendance system is a technology that uses facial recognition algorithms to identify individuals and record their attendance. It works by capturing an image or video of an individual's face, analyzing the facial features and comparing them with the existing database of faces to determine if there is a match. If a match is found, the system marks the individual as present and records the attendance. There are several benefits of using a face recognition-based attendance system. It eliminates the need for physical attendance registers, reduces the time required for attendance tracking, and increases accuracy by eliminating errors associated with manual attendance tracking. With our system, you can track attendance accurately and efficiently, reduce errors, and improve productivity.
