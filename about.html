Welcome to our facial recognition system! We are a small team of engineering students who have built this system to simplify attendance tracking in college. Our goal is to provide a reliable and efficient way for professors and administrators to mark attendance while also reducing the workload and time spent on traditional methods.

Our team understands the importance of data privacy and security, which is why we have implemented various measures to ensure that all personal information is kept safe and confidential. We follow strict data protection protocols and adhere to all relevant data protection regulations.
Our facial recognition system has been thoroughly tested and optimized to perform well in a variety of lighting and environmental conditions. It can also be easily integrated with existing attendance tracking systems, making it a cost-effective solution for colleges and universities.
We are proud to have developed a system that helps streamline attendance tracking in educational institutions. Our facial recognition system has already been adopted by several colleges, and we are committed to continuously improving and updating it to meet the evolving needs of the education sector.
